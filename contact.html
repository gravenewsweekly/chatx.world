📧 How to Contact Us

For any inquiries, support requests, or feedback, please email us at:

handedaksh@gmail.com

📝 What to Include in Your Email

When sending an email, please **mention the following details**:

  • 🔹 **Subject Line** – Clearly state the purpose (e.g., "Account Issue," "Report User," "General Inquiry").
  • 🔹 **Your Chatx.world Username** – If applicable, include your username.
  • 🔹 **Detailed Description** – Explain the issue or question in detail.
  • 🔹 **Screenshots (if necessary)** – Attach any relevant screenshots to help us understand the issue.
  • 🔹 **Your Preferred Contact Method** – Let us know if you need a reply and how we can respond.

🚨 Reporting Issues

If you are reporting a **rule violation, harassment, or technical problem**, please include:

  • 📌 **Username of the offending user** (if applicable).
  • 📌 **Date & Time of Incident** (mention when it happened).
  • 📌 **Chatroom Name** (if it occurred in a specific chatroom).
  • 📌 **Screenshots or Evidence** (if possible, attach proof).

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

⏳ Response Time

We try to respond to all emails **within 24-48 hours**. Please be patient while we review your request.

If your issue is urgent, mark your subject line as "**URGENT: [Issue]**" for priority handling.
