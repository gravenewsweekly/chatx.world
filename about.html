🌍 What is Chatx.world?

Chatx.world is a **100% anonymous chatroom** where you can instantly connect with strangers **without sign-ups, personal data, or tracking**.

Designed with a **Windows 98-inspired aesthetic**, it brings back the **nostalgic feel of early internet chatrooms**.

💡 Why Chatx.world Exists

Modern chat platforms demand **emails, logins, and even subscriptions**. Chatx.world changes that by offering **instant access with no hassle**.

Just **choose a nickname and start chatting**—**no ads, no tracking, just real conversations**.

💬 Features

  • 🔹 100% Anonymous – No registration needed.
  • 🔹 Real-time Chat – Instant message delivery.
  • 🔹 Live User Count – See who's online.
  • 🔹 Retro Windows 98 Theme – A nostalgic experience.
  • 🔹 Secure & Moderated – A safe space, free from spam.

🚀 Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a **simple, safe, and fun** chat experience by prioritizing:

  • ✅ Privacy – No tracking or data collection.
  • ✅ Security – Moderated and safe conversations.
  • ✅ Accessibility – One-click chat experience.

⚠️ Chat Safety Guidelines

To keep Chatx.world safe and fun, follow these simple rules:

  • 🚫 **No sharing personal information** (name, address, etc.).
  • 🔞 **No explicit or offensive content.**
  • 🚷 **No bullying, spam, or harassment.**
  • ❌ **Rule-breakers will be permanently banned.**

💻 Technologies Used

  • 🖥️ **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • 🔥 **Backend:** Firebase (for real-time chat)
  • 🌍 **Live Messaging:** WebSockets
  • 🔐 **Security:** Moderation tools to maintain a safe chat environment
