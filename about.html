🌍 What is Chatx.world?

Chatx.world is an anonymous chatroom designed for people who love chatting freely with strangers—no sign-ups, no personal data, just pure conversation.

With a Windows 98-inspired design, it brings back the nostalgic feel of early internet chatrooms.

💡 Why Was Chatx.world Created?

Most chat platforms today require sign-ups, emails, and even payment for premium features. Chatx.world takes a different approach—offering instant access with no hassle.

Here, all you need to do is pick a nickname and start chatting. No tracking, no ads, just real conversations.

💬 Features of Chatx.world

  • 🔹 100% Anonymous – No registration needed.
  • 🔹 Real-time Chat – Instant message delivery.
  • 🔹 Live User Count – See how many people are online.
  • 🔹 Permanent Messages – Messages stay forever.
  • 🔹 Retro Windows 98 Theme – Brings back old-school vibes.
  • 🔹 Safe & Moderated – No spam, no abuse.

🚀 Our Mission

Chatx.world aims to create a fun, safe, and nostalgic chat experience. We prioritize:

  • ✅ Privacy – No tracking, no data collection.
  • ✅ Security – A safe space for all users.
  • ✅ Simplicity – One-click access to real conversations.

Bringing back the golden age of chatting!

⚠️ Chat Safety Guidelines

To keep Chatx.world safe and fun for everyone, follow these simple rules:

  • 🚫 No sharing of personal information (real name, address, etc.).
  • 🔞 No explicit or offensive content.
  • 🚷 No bullying, spamming, or harassment.
  • ❌ Violators will be permanently banned.
