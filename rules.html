⚠️ Chatx.world Community Rules

To maintain a **safe and friendly** chat environment, all users must follow these **strict guidelines**. Violating these rules may result in a **permanent ban**.

🚫 Prohibited Behavior

  • ❌ **No Hate Speech or Harassment** – Be respectful to others.
  • ❌ **No Personal Attacks** – No bullying, threats, or abusive language.
  • ❌ **No NSFW or Explicit Content** – No adult content, nudity, or inappropriate messages.
  • ❌ **No Spamming or Advertising** – Do not flood the chat or promote links.
  • ❌ **No Impersonation** – Do not pretend to be someone else.
  • ❌ **No Illegal Activities** – Discussion of drugs, hacking, fraud, or other illegal actions is forbidden.

🔒 Privacy & Safety

  • ✅ **Do Not Share Personal Information** – Keep your real name, phone number, or address private.
  • ✅ **Use a Nickname** – Stay anonymous and avoid using real identities.
  • ✅ **Report Suspicious Behavior** – If you see someone breaking the rules, report them immediately.
  • ✅ **Stay Cautious** – Never trust strangers with personal data.

👮 Moderation & Enforcement

  • 🔹 Our **moderators** actively monitor chatrooms.
  • 🔹 Violations may result in **temporary or permanent bans**.
  • 🔹 Appeals can be submitted through the **Contact Page**.
  • 🔹 **Repeated offenders** will be permanently blacklisted.

📝 Guidelines for a Better Chat Experience

  • ✅ **Be Respectful** – Treat everyone with kindness.
  • ✅ **Engage in Meaningful Conversations** – Avoid toxic arguments.
  • ✅ **Keep It Friendly** – This is a safe space for everyone.
  • ✅ **Follow the Rules** – Help us maintain a fun and positive chatroom.

🔔 Consequences of Violating Rules

Failure to comply with these rules may lead to:

  • 🚨 **First Offense** – A warning.
  • 🚨 **Second Offense** – Temporary ban (24-72 hours).
  • 🚨 **Third Offense** – Permanent ban with no appeal.
  • 🚨 **Serious Violations** – Immediate and irreversible ban.

🛠️ Need Help?

If you need assistance or want to **report an issue**, visit our Contact Page. Our support team will respond as soon as possible.
