⚠️ Chat Guidelines

To keep Chatx.world **safe, fun, and respectful**, all users must follow these rules. Breaking them may result in **warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal** from the platform.

🚫 What’s NOT Allowed

  • ❌ **Hate Speech & Harassment** – No racism, sexism, or bullying.
  • ❌ **Personal Attacks** – No insults, threats, or abusive messages.
  • ❌ **NSFW Content** – No nudity, sexual content, or explicit messages.
  • ❌ **Spam & Advertising** – No flood messages, self-promotion, or ads.
  • ❌ **Impersonation** – Do not pretend to be someone else.
  • ❌ **Illegal Activities** – No discussion of drugs, hacking, or fraud.

🔒 Privacy & Safety

  • ✅ **Keep Personal Info Private** – No sharing real names, phone numbers, or addresses.
  • ✅ **Use a Nickname** – Stay anonymous to protect your identity.
  • ✅ **Report Issues** – Report rule violations to moderators.
  • ✅ **Stay Cautious** – Don’t trust strangers with private data.

👮 Moderation & Enforcement

  • 🔹 **Moderators** monitor the chat and enforce rules.
  • 🔹 **Violations** may result in warnings, bans, or permanent removal.
  • 🔹 **Appeals** can be submitted via the **Contact Page**.
  • 🔹 **Repeat Offenders** will be permanently blacklisted.

🔔 Consequences of Violating Rules

Breaking the rules will result in:

  • 🚨 **First Offense** – A warning.
  • 🚨 **Second Offense** – A temporary ban (24-72 hours).
  • 🚨 **Third Offense** – A permanent ban with no appeal.
  • 🚨 **Severe Violations** – Immediate and irreversible ban.

🛠️ Need Help?

If you need assistance, have concerns, or wish to report an issue, please visit our Contact Page. Our support team will respond as soon as possible.
